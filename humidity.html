Humidity

As expected, the humidity begins to drop as one moves away from the equator(0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the cluster of 0% humidity along the North Pole as opposed to non-zero humidty in the southpole This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
